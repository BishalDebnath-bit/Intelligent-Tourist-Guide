ITG — Intelligent Tourist Guide — was built as a web programming project with one simple idea: every traveller deserves better information than what bloated, ad-ridden platforms provide.

We believe the best travel experiences come from local knowledge, honest curation, and smart technology — not sponsored results. ITG puts hand-picked destinations front and centre, with all the detail you need and none of the noise you don't.

Why We Exist

Built on Three Principles

Every design and content decision in ITG flows from these three core ideas.

Honest Curation

No sponsored listings. No paid placements. Every destination on ITG is included purely on the merit of its experience and visitor value.

Depth Over Breadth

Fewer spots with richer information beats a hundred shallow listings. We go deep on every destination with maps, entry info, and seasonal advice.

Smart Discovery

Real-time search, category filtering, and contextual nearby suggestions help you find the right destination for your travel style, fast.
